NBA Player heights from 2008-9
Description
Heights of all NBA players from the 2008-9 season.

Usage
nba_heights
Format
A data frame with 435 observations (players) on the following 4 variables.

last_name
Last name.

first_name
First name.

h_meters
Height, in meters.

h_in
Height, in inches.

Source
Collected from http://www.nba.com.
